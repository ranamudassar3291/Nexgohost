Build a complete **professional WHMCS-style hosting billing panel** with full Admin Panel and Client Area. The system must be production-ready and structured like WHMCS.

Use **Node.js + Express backend** and **MySQL or JSON database**, with modern responsive frontend (Bootstrap or Tailwind).

---

# 1️⃣ ADMIN PANEL (/admin)

Create a full admin control panel with these sections and buttons:

### Dashboard

Route: /admin/dashboard
Show statistics:

* Total Clients
* Active Orders
* Pending Orders
* Paid Invoices
* Unpaid Invoices
* Tickets Open
* Monthly Revenue
